archer fined

Manchester:The England and Wales Cricket board has officially warned speedster Jofra Archer and fined him an'undisclosed amount' for breaching the bio security protocals ahed of the second test versus West Indies.

Archer broke the bio-bubblelast Mon day and left for his home in Brighton.

"Following a disciplinary hearing held on Fry day evening,17 july,england cricketer Jofra Archer fined undisclosed amount and recieved an official written warning after admitting to breaching the team's bio secure protocols on monday 13 julywhen he made anunauthorised visit to his home in Hove," the ECB said in a statement.
